Churn

Is Unavoidable

So Learning should be a primary focus

The goal being to learn what matters even if you drop the framework or approach.

Angular 1.x is ok, but Angular 2.x is better on this front.

React's approach is very sound by leaving aspects up to you.

Balance need to learn vs. productivity well

Understand what learning is best for

you

your team

all developers

people who want to become developers

people who don't want to become developers

Throw Things Away

Design to throw things away... this is the common case.

For your own code or for frameworks and libraries

You will always come away with experience and understanding

It's harder (mentally) to throw away monolithic things...

but it is cleaner

Modularize

  • Allow things to be small and decoupled
  • Are often tied to a specific environment
  • Which excludes others

Goal is to minimize dependencies.

But decide and live with it.

Because you should be able to throw it away.
